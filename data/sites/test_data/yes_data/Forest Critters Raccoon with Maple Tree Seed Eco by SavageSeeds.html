    Forest Critter - Raccoon - Made to Order

    This cuddly raccoon has been chasing Maple Tree seeds or “helicopters” all day and is about to be surprised by the one landing on his back.

    All Forest Critters are Eco Friendly.
    Handmade from environmentally friendly, quality Wool felt & Eco Felt, stuffed with
    Bamboo Nature- Fil Fiber. Soft and cuddly for you and safe for the Earth.
    No buttons or small parts.

    9” tall 7” at its tail & widest part
    Body in light brown. Tail & face detail in white, black and browns. Maple Tree seed "helicopter" in olive green with brown embroidery spiral detail.

    Each purchase comes with a gift!
    You will receive plantable paper hearts embedded with wildflower seeds & planting instructions. A fun project to encourage love for our planet & bring a bit of color to your window sill or garden.(Sorry, wildflower seeds not available internationally)

    All handmade Forest Critters are made with love and attention to detail using quality materials in a smoke and pet free studio.
    Shipped in gift ready packaging. Personal message available in handmade card by request.

    This made to order item will be made and ready to ship within 7-14 business days.
    Your item may vary slightly from the one pictured due to its handmade nature.

    Spot clean only
    Ages 3 & up

    *Proud to be featured in Mollie Makes Magazine - Issue 21- December 2012*
