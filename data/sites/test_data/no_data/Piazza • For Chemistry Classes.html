Features Customized for Chemistry

  • Piazza has a robust LaTeX equation editor for chemical formulas and mathematical expressions
  • The Piazza mobile apps let students upload photos from their phones, which is excellent for documenting issues or results from laboratory experiments
  • Piazza allows students to gain clarity on questions and problems - removing ambiguity and confusion outside the classroom
  • Piazza supports multimedia embedding, so you and your students can share diagrams, images, and videos
  • Our in-class polls feature allows for in-class quizzes and feedback
  • Instructors can upload homework assignments and other resources to our course pages
